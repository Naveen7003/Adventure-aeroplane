Blue River Aviation provides you with the resources necessary to
access the vastness of the Last Frontier. Whether you are looking
for hunting, fishing, camping, or any other type of adventure, we
will get you there. With a variety of aircraft in our fleet, we are

About us

Everyone should
have their head in
the clouds.

Blue River Aviation’s founder, Jamie Hastings, began flight
instructing in 2009 while working as a pilot for Peninsula Airways.
After a decade of flight instruction, he decided to pursue his flight
school full-time and bring on a number of other instructors. Thus,
Blue River Aviation was born.

Our flight school focuses on professional aviation training,
preparing students for everything from airline careers to off-airport
in Alaska’s vast backcountry. We take pilots from zero hours to
commercial multi-engine and CFI. In addition, we provide tailwheel,
seaplane, and ski instruction.
